Book of Judges — Chapter Summaries & Cycle Stages

Concise, chapter-by-chapter overview: content, characters, lessons, and cycle stage.

Chapter Main Content Main Characters Lessons Cycle Stage
1 Israel fails to drive out Canaanites Judah, Simeon, Canaanites Partial obedience brings lasting trouble Sin / Incomplete Obedience
2 Cycle of sin introduced Angel of the Lord, Joshua, Israel Forgetting God leads to oppression; repentance brings help Cycle Introduced
3 First judges deliver Israel Othniel, Ehud, Shamgar God raises unlikely deliverers Deliverance / Peace
4 Deborah & Barak defeat Sisera Deborah, Barak, Jael God uses men and women for His purposes Deliverance
5 Song of Deborah Deborah, Barak Give glory to God for victories Peace / Praise
6 Gideon called Gideon, Angel of the Lord God equips the weak when they obey Oppression → Repentance
7 Gideon defeats Midianites Gideon, 300 men God saves by His power, not numbers Deliverance
8 Gideon rules; Israel turns to idols Gideon, Midianite kings Success can lead to pride and idolatry Peace → Decline
9 Abimelech’s violent rule Abimelech, Jotham Selfish ambition destroys Sin / Decline
10 Israel returns to idols Tola, Jair, Israel Repeated sin brings repeated judgment Sin → Oppression
11 Jephthah defeats Ammonites Jephthah, Ammonites Be careful with vows; God uses outcasts Deliverance
12 Civil strife; minor judges Jephthah, Ibzan, Elon, Abdon Pride and division harm God’s people Decline (Internal Strife)
13 Birth of Samson foretold Samson’s parents, Angel God sets apart deliverers before birth Preparation
14 Samson’s marriage Samson, Philistines Compromise with the world brings strife Sin / Compromise
15 Samson’s revenge Samson, Philistines God empowers imperfect people Deliverance (partial)
16 Samson betrayed; dies destroying Philistines Samson, Delilah, Philistines Sin enslaves, but repentance brings victory Deliverance (through death)
17 Micah’s idols Micah, Levite False worship corrupts community Sin / Idolatry
18 Danites seize idols Danites, Micah, Levite Idolatry spreads when unchecked Sin / Idolatry
19 Levite’s concubine abused and killed Levite, concubine, Gibeah men Moral collapse without God’s law Moral Decline
20 Civil war with Benjamin Israel tribes, Benjamites Sin within God’s people brings destruction Oppression (internal)
21 Aftermath of war; solutions tried Israel tribes, Benjamin Human fixes to sin are flawed; need God’s king Decline
